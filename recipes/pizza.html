Homemade Pizza Recipe

Home

Description

Mix a simple yeast-leavened dough until smooth, let it rise until doubled, then stretch it into a round or rectangle. Spread with tangy tomato sauce, scatter shredded mozzarella and your favorite toppings in an even layer. Bake on a preheated stone or baking sheet at high heat until the crust is crisp and golden, then finish with fresh basil and a drizzle of olive oil.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Active dry yeast
  • Warm water
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine flour, yeast, sugar, and salt; add warm water and olive oil, then stir until a shaggy dough forms.
  2. Turn onto a lightly floured surface and knead 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic; place in an oiled bowl, cover, and let rise 1–1½ hours until doubled.
  3. Preheat your oven to 250 °C (475 °F) with a pizza stone or inverted baking sheet on the middle rack.
  4. Punch down the dough, shape it into a 30 cm (12 in) round (or rectangle), and transfer to a piece of parchment paper or a peel dusted with semolina.
  5. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the surface, sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan, and add any other toppings you like.
  6. Slide the pizza (on parchment) onto the hot stone/sheet and bake 10–12 minutes until the crust is blistered; remove, top with fresh basil and a drizzle of olive oil, then slice and serve.
