Lasagna Recipe

Home

Description

Layer sheets of tender lasagna noodles with a rich meat-tomato sauce, creamy béchamel, and a generous sprinkle of mozzarella and Parmesan, then repeat to build three or four cheesy tiers. Bake at 190 °C (375 °F) for 35–40 minutes until bubbling and golden, let rest 10 minutes, slice and serve garnished with fresh basil.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 190 °C (375 °F) and lightly grease a 9×13-inch baking dish
  2. In a large skillet, sauté chopped onion and minced garlic in olive oil until translucent, then add ground beef and cook until browned; drain any excess fat
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, season with salt, pepper, and Italian herbs, then simmer for 10–15 minutes to deepen the flavors.
  4. Meanwhile, make the béchamel by melting butter in a saucepan, whisking in flour to form a roux, then gradually whisking in warm milk until thickened; season with a pinch of nutmeg, salt, and pepper.
  5. Assemble the lasagna by layering noodles, meat sauce, béchamel, and shredded mozzarella (plus a sprinkle of Parmesan), repeating for three or four layers, and finishing with remaining cheeses on top.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, remove foil and bake another 10–15 minutes until golden and bubbly, then let rest 10 minutes before slicing.
